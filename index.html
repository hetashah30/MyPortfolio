GrowBridge

I developed a streamlined platform to support the SME sector by facilitating access to small loans, thereby fostering innovation and bolstering the ecosystem. The platform features a user-friendly web form for loan applications, allowing users to register, log in, explore special business loan offers, and apply for loans. Additionally, users can check their credit and CIBIL scores to determine eligibility before proceeding to the sanction form. The platform is enhanced with a sophisticated AI chatbot, providing instant assistance and addressing queries related to the loan process and business financing.

JusticeDox

JusticeDox is an intuitive online platform exclusively designed for police officials, offering streamlined evidence submission and case management. Through secure login credentials and two-factor authentication, officers can upload evidentiary materials directly to the system, ensuring authenticity with digital signatures. The portal's user-friendly interface simplifies case tracking and collaboration, enabling efficient investigation management. With stringent data privacy measures in place, JusticeDox provides a secure and centralized solution for law enforcement agencies to uphold professionalism and efficiency in their investigative endeavors.
